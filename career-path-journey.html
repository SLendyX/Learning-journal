My frontend developer career path journey is almost halfway!

After several failed attempts to learn frontend development I finnaly found the perfect platform to teach me what I need to make the perfect site that I desire.

How I stay commited to learning

I do have and have had a peculiar interest to learning new things especially those based around computer science and programming languages. Even though I do have this burning desire to learn more it's not strong enough to make me learn consistently throughout the day.

As such I have created this journal so I can keep track of my journey, and in this way I keep accountable for the dayly working load.

How I got started

I started off gradually, filling up each article with the notes that I took throughout the courses and tried as best as I could to explain those concepts in my own words in order to fully grasp the main idea.

Even though this site started off as a personal project, it got a lot of attention from other people interested in frontend development and I am happy that I could help too.
